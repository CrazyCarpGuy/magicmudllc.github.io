Magic Mud LLC

Magic Mud LLC develops and licenses proprietary surface-conditioning material for baseballs and footballs.

Our work has focused on replicating and standardizing traditional ball-prep rubbing mud through controlled sourcing and process development. The resulting material has been validated to meet the handling, grip, and performance expectations of professional play.

We operate as a development and licensing entity and do not engage in public retail distribution.

Magic Mud LLC

Magic Mud LLC develops and licenses proprietary surface-conditioning compounds for professional baseballs.

Our work focuses on replicating and standardizing traditional ball-preparation methods through controlled sourcing and process development. The resulting materials are designed to meet the handling, grip, and performance expectations of professional play.

We operate as a development and licensing entity and do not engage in public retail distribution.
